Shayana Farm began operations in 2002. Today, Shayana Farm is a group of companies headquartered in the Republic of Uzbekistan.

In the global pharmaceutical markets, Shayana Farm seeks to maintain and strengthen its leadership in the segment of quality and vital drugs for the treatment of various diseases (Oncology, Antibiotics, Parenteral Nutrition Preparations, Plasma Substitutes, Hepatoprotectors, etc.). The company achieves this by increasing the efficiency of production processes, adapting to the global and local markets, and by presenting its products in economically viable markets. Shayana Farm's short-term strategy is to pool intellectual property assets and increase their value to shareholders and customers. The long-term strategy of the company is to become one of the leading players in the markets of presence, due to natural growth and growth through the creation of joint ventures.
We strive to always remain a successful pharmaceutical company providing effective drugs that improve the quality of life, and to be a company that is respected for its professionalism and which has a reputation as a reliable partner, supplier and employer.
